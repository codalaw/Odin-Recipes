Lunch: Chicken Caesar Salad Wrap

A portable, flavorful lunch wrap combining tender chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. Crunchy, savory, and perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients (for 2 wraps):

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup Caesar dressing
  • ½ cup croutons (optional)
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling: In a bowl, combine chicken, lettuce, Parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing, and croutons. Toss until evenly coated.
  2. Warm tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry pan over medium heat for 10–15 seconds per side to make them pliable.
  3. Assemble wraps: Place half of the salad mixture in the center of each tortilla. Fold in the sides and roll tightly.
  4. Serve: Cut diagonally and serve immediately, or wrap in foil for later.
